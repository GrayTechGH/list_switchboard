  • Short Name: Stoker Award Type Record # 40
  • Full Name: Bram Stoker Award
  • Awarded For: Horror
  • Awarded By: Members of the Horror Writers Association (HWA)
  • Poll: No
  • Covers more than just SF: No
  • Webpages: horror.org, Science Fiction Awards Database, Wikipedia-EN
  • Note: Sub-Genre Award (Horror). Presented annually by the Horror Writers Association (HWA) for "superior achievement" in horror writing. The winners are selected by ballot of the Active members of the HWA. The awards are named after the influential Irish horror writer Bram Stoker, author of the novel Dracula, among others. Awards listed by year of eligibility.

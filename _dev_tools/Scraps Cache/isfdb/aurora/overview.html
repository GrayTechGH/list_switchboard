  • Short Name: Aurora Award Type Record # 5
  • Full Name: Prix Aurora Awards
  • Awarded For: Best Canadian science fiction and fantasy works and activities in English and French
  • Awarded By: Canadian SF and Fantasy Association at Canvention
  • Poll: No
  • Covers more than just SF: No
  • Webpages: prix-aurora-awards.ca, Science Fiction Awards Database, sentex.net, Wikipedia-EN, Wikipedia-FR, zinewiki.com
  • Note: National Award. The Prix Aurora Awards are given out annually for the best Canadian science fiction and fantasy literary works, artworks, fan activities from that year, and are awarded in both English and French. The event is organized by Canvention and the awards are given out by the Canadian SF and Fantasy Association. The Award has been called the Prix Aurora since 1991 The Award was titled "Coeurl Award" in 1980 From 1981 through 1985 it was titled the "CSFFA Award". From 1986 through 1990 it was titled the "Prix Casper" After 2010 the French language categories were merged with the Prix Aurora-Boréal and were awarded separately.
